How accurate is the pricing?

Every deal is priced against recent Grailed sold data using time-weighted averaging — recent sales count more. We adjust for condition, size, and season. "High confidence" means 5+ verified comps. We don't guess.

The best deals sell within minutes. We send alerts the moment our system identifies them. Enable push notifications on Telegram for instant delivery. Speed wins in resale.

Every item goes through a 7-signal authentication system: text analysis, price plausibility, seller reputation, listing quality, image analysis, brand-specific markers, and cross-reference detection. Items scoring below 65% are blocked.
